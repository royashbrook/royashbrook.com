institutions already put scores on you. credit, risk, insurability, a dozen others, mostly with rules you never get to see. lifescored is me rebuilding that idea inside out: every rule shown with its citation, every weight visible and editable, nothing hidden behind a model you can't inspect.

there's no server doing the scoring. you download the rules and the math runs locally, so you can see exactly what moves your number and why, and change the assumptions if you disagree with them.

have a look: [lifescored.com](https://lifescored.com). it's open source too: [github.com/royashbrook/lifescored](https://github.com/royashbrook/lifescored).
